About Me

Hello! My name is Natalie La. I am a creative engineer located in the greater Los Angeles, CA area. With my experiences and skill sets in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I am driven to empower organizations through creative coding. Some of my past works and current projects have been displayed in this online portfolio. As Project Manager, I am responsible for managing the project schedule, budget, scope, communication, risk, and stakeholders. "Kuma" means "bear" in Japanese. Bears are known for their strength, courage, and empathetic characteristics. As "Natakkuma", these charateristics have allowed me to lead and work in teams of diverse personalities and talents to resolve a variety of project issues in order to meet project milestones and deadlines. I strongly believe in opportunities for improvement and growth, and am always open to new and exciting challenges.
